Talks & Lectures

This is a list of moments in which I talked about math

Research talks

  • A Feigin-Frenkel theorem with n singularities. I held various seminars in different occasions where I explained the results of my article "A Feigin Frenkel theorem with n singularities". Respectively: at an international event on infinite dimensional Lie algebras held in Rome, during the conference "Interactions between Mathematics and Physics" in honor of Victor J. Kac and finally at the algebra and geometry seminar at the University of Bologna.

Introductory talks

  • A tour of the representation theory of the affine algebra at the critical level, Winter meeting in Algebra and Geometry in Rome 2023.An occasion in which I told the story of the Feigin-Frenkel theorem about the center at the critical level and the Frenkel-Gaitsgory theorem about spherical representations of the affine algebra at the critical level.

Classes

  • Cycle of seminars on Geometric representation theory Rome, Spring-Summer 2024. I was the main lecturer of a cycle of seminars on Geometric Representation theory. I introduced basic material with a view towards the Geomtric Langlands Conjectures. We treated mainly treated Geometric Function Theory and the Geometric Satake correspondence.
